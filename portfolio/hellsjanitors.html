Hell's Janitors


A multiplayer zombie shooter. I wrote this in junior year high school in C#, XNA. This is still available on game jolt

Players can set and activate traps, spend points, and use abilities. They can place entities in the world that attack zombies or assist allies.

The zombies needed to be able to path from the dirt-holes they crawl out of, to wherever the player is. Each tile in the world is treated as a node linked to its neighboring tiles. An A* search is performed until the zombie can freely move towards the player without bumping into anything. An additional constraint is that certain areas are unreachable by the player until those areas are unlocked. To account for this, each time a door is opened or glass gets broken, the dirt holes perform a path find to the player to see if they should activate.

Featuring four player splitscreen, the goal was to survive

The game shipped with an editor for players to make and share levels. Every level in the game was built using this editor. Tools are valuable!
