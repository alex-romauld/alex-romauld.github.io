About

Hello Visitor,

I'm a Georgia Tech OMSCS student and game developer. With my free time I work on experimental games designed to test new theories and challenge foundational assumptions. I want to build games that are generous to their audience, value simplicity, and require the player to adapt and exercise skill. Games that surprise and delight, function as a harmoneous experience, and do a lot with a little. If you're someone not fond of many decisions modern games make, of the way they try to sell themseleves to you ... If you find current trends to be anti-artisan and distasteful; or just wanting to try something different, then you might want to stick around.

Thank you for your time,
